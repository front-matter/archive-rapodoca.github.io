What's Up With Chempedia?

By Richard L. Apodaca

March 11, 2009

You may have noticed that Chempedia is now offline, replaced by a proxy page. The service is being radically redesigned in response to feedback from many individuals over the last year or so. The Web has created unprecedented opportunities for rethinking scientific publication; the goal is to use Chempedia as an adaptable resource for advancing this idea.

You can read more about Chempedia and its possible future on the Metamolecular Product Blog.
